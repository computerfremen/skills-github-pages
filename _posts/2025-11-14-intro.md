So, here I am, in front of a blank text editor, trying to get the first line of my blog's project going. I'm not a native English speaker, as I'm sure you've noticed, but I'm starting this space to try to achieve two goals:
- To dump all my ideas in a non-volatile space.
Now that this entry has made me think about it, I've realized that the way I like to take notes sometimes turns into something mandatory. I start to feel like I'd miss out on ideas and emotions if I didn't take notes. I started using the iPhone's notes a few years ago to journal, record ideas on the bus, and write down things I couldn't say. - To improve my English skills.
